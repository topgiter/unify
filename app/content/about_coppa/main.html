COPPA

COPPA is the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We strive to comply with COPPA. If you are the parent of a child under the age of thirteen (13) who has created an account using a false birth date, please notify our support center and upon completion of our investigation we will immediately cancel your child's account and delete all materials associated with your child's account from the site and our database. For more information about COPPA, visit the Federal Trade Commission's web site.

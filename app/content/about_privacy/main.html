Privacy Policy

We know your privacy on the internet is extremely important to you. Since GS Poetry gathers certain personal information about registered users, we want to ensure that you fully understand the terms and conditions surrounding the use of the information collected. GS Poetry reserves the right to revise this policy at any time without notification to you, so please read it often in connection with your use of the GS Poetry web site.

Our Pledge to You

We know that you care how information about you is used and shared.

  • GS Poetry will not share your e-mail address or other identifiable information with any third party company (no external spam).
  • GS Poetry will protect your information through the use of secure, encrypted databases.

Personally Identifiable Information

GS Poetry collects the personally identifiable information that you submit on the registration and user profile forms to permit you access to certain content on the site.

Generally, the information GS Poetry collects helps administartors to communicate with you regarding the status of your poetry submissions, any issues that may need to be resolved or to answer any other types of questions. For instance, we use your e-mail address to provide you with an e-mail confirmation of your registration or poetry submission. In addition, users on the site will be able to communicate with you via the G-Soul Café, site's forum.

We will not share your personally-identifiable information with any entity, except to the the proper local, state or federal authorities when necessary to comply with the law (i.e. answering subpoenas) and at the user's permission.


Password Recommendations

As we live in a world where digital theft is increasingly rampant. We recommend that you choose a password that will not be easily guessed by others. It is recommended that you use a separate password for every site to which you are a member. This is a form of damage control. In case the security of one site is compromised, your other accounts are still protected. If you forget your password, you can reset it on the Forgot Password page.


Use of Automatic Information and Cookies

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your web browser to enable GS Poetry to recognize your browser and to provide features that recognize your account as logged on and other site features. Further, to help us tailor the site to the needs and interests of users, or for other purposes, we may gather aggregate, non-personally identifiable information via cookies. Examples include the type of Internet browser visitors are using, the frequency of visits to GS Poetry and which pages visitors viewed. GS Poetry also uses cookies for voting in the polls and the poetry rating system. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many major web sites use them. Cookies stored on your computer that are designated for GS Poetry.com use, can not be retrieved by other web sites.


Minors on GS Poetry

Children under thirteen (13) years of age are not allowed to register on the site. We do strive to comply with the the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If you are the parent of a child under the age of thirteen (13) who has created an account using a false birth date, please notify the GS Poetry Support Team and we will immediately cancel your child's account and delete all materials associated with your child's account from the site and our database. Anyone legally under the age of thirteen (13) who registers on the site is violating our Terms of Use. GS Poetry is not liable for any actions taken by a violator of our age requirement. For more information about COPPA, visit the Federal Trade Commission's web site.


Updating or Deleting Your Information

Deleting your profile is controlled by you. Deleting your profile does not remove your poems from the site or the database. If you would like your poems removed from the site, please contact us. All requests will be processed within 48 hours.


Security

We understand that many people are concerned about security on the Internet. The security of the information that you provide to us is also very important to us. To prevent unauthorized disclosure, maintain data accuracy and ensure the correct use of information, we have in place reasonable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure any information we collect online. Purchases are made through PayPal. Once you are on PayPal’s website, you are subject to the terms of use and privacy policies of PayPal. In the event that the security system is breached, we will attempt to notify members as soon as possible.


Links, Hosted Sites & Advertisers

We do not have any control over the privacy practices of any external websites linked from the main www.gspoetry.com domain (or any of the websites that are hosted on the server and aren't affiliated with GS Poetry). Therefore you should review their privacy policies separately from GS Poetry's and know that we in no way have any control over their practices.

Third party advertisers may collect certain information that is available to them through your interaction with their advertisements. GS Poetry has no control or knowledge as to their practices and it is suggested that you read their privacy policy separately before clicking on any advertisements.

Copyrights

The Digital Millennium Copyright Act of 1998 amended current copyright laws to take into account various new digital technologies. GS Poetry strives to be compliant with the guidelines outlined in the DMCA. Visit the DMCA Wikipedia page for more detailed information.

If you believe that any material on this web site infringes upon any copyright which you own or control, contact GSP support of such infringement.

If you are interested in officially copyrighting your poems, you have to submit an application to the United States Copyright Office in Washington D.C. The links below provide a good starting point for registering your poetry with the office. There is a small fee to register your works.
